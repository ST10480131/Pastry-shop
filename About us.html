About Sweet Craving Pastry Shop

Our Story

Sweet Craving Pastry Shop was founded in 2015 in Cape Town. It began as a small bakery serving friends and neighbors. Due to growing demand, an official store opened in 2018, now delighting both loyal customers and tourists with high-quality baked goods.
Every visit is a delicious escape into a world of flaky pastries and rich cakes. From golden croissants to indulgent Black Forest cakes, we blend tradition, flavour, and creativity to make every bite unforgettable.

Our Mission

We strive to make pastries so delicious that each bite brings joy. By enhancing the emotional experience through flavor and tradition, we honor classic baking while creating new delights for our customers.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become Cape Town’s most trusted and loved pastry shop, blending tradition, innovation, and a seamless customer experience that delights food lovers, families, tourists, and event planners.

Why Choose Us?

  • Fresh ingredients, baked daily
  • Custom cake orders for every occasion
  • Friendly service with a smile
  • Locally sourced and organic where possible
